# GIF Storyboard

Use this if you want a short social-ready GIF for GitHub, X, or LinkedIn.

## Target Length

- `10-14s`

## Recording Goal

Show the product value in one pass:

1. understand candidate-to-role fit
2. compare opportunities
3. prepare for a real interview

## Shot Order

### Shot 1: Hero + Sidebar

- Duration: `2s`
- Frame: app landing state with sidebar visible
- Purpose: establish that this is a clean, local demo
- Caption: `A GitHub-safe job agent demo`

### Shot 2: Fit Snapshot

- Duration: `3-4s`
- Action: scroll just enough to show score, component bars, and strong matches
- Purpose: show fast role-fit judgment
- Caption: `See role fit, risk, and positioning at a glance`

### Shot 3: Offer Comparison

- Duration: `3-4s`
- Action: click `Offer Comparison`, pause on ranked shortlist
- Purpose: show prioritization, not generic text generation
- Caption: `Prioritize the right opportunities first`

### Shot 4: Interview Prep Brief

- Duration: `3-4s`
- Action: click `Interview Prep Brief`, pause on likely questions and story map
- Purpose: end on actionability
- Caption: `Turn a role into a prep-ready interview brief`

## Capture Notes

- Use one sample candidate only
- Keep cursor movement slow and deliberate
- Do not scroll too far
- Avoid showing setup steps
- Record at desktop width

## Export Notes

- Prefer `1440px` wide recording, export down if needed
- Keep file under `10 MB` if the destination matters
- Add captions after recording if you want a cleaner loop

## Suggested Posting Copy

`A small public demo for a larger job-search assistant: fit judgment, shortlist prioritization, and interview prep, without exposing the private orchestration layer.`
